About the course

Welcome to the MERN Stack Full Stack Development Course designed specifically for students in the United Kingdom who have recently migrated for postgraduate studies in other subjects. This comprehensive three-month course is perfect for individuals with no prior coding knowledge who are eager to learn full-stack development and pursue careers in the information technology sector in the UK.

Beginner-Friendly:

No prior coding knowledge or experience is required. Our course is designed to cater to students with diverse academic backgrounds, providing a supportive learning environment for beginners.

Online Learning :

Our course is fully conducted online, allowing you to learn at your own pace from the comfort of your home. Access course materials, lectures, and assignments through our user-friendly online platform.

Cohort Model:

Joining the course means becoming part of a select group. The first batch is limited to only 12 slots, ensuring personalized attention and a collaborative learning experience. You will have one main mentor and three program counselors who will guide you throughout your software development journey

Comprehensive
Curriculum:

Our course covers all aspects of full stack web development using the MERN stack - MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js. From the fundamentals to advanced topics, you will gain a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end development.

Course Add-on's

In addition to MERN stack development, we offer supplementary sessions on UI/UX design, SEO (Search Engine Optimization), resume building, and LinkedIn outreach. These skills will enhance your profile and increase your chances of success in the IT industry.
